How to play the Butterfly Memory Game:


  • click 'play' in the navigation bar at the top, or below these instructions.

  • click the button to start the game.

  • level 1 will start; one of the butterflies will flash a colour. Think, or say aloud, what that butterfly's name is, and click on that butterfly.

  • level 2 will flash two images. Again, click on the butterflies that flash. When they flash, think what their names are.

  • keep clicking on the order of butterflies that flash. As you think of their names, this will help you to remember their names.
